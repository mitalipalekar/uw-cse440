Bringing Parents and Babies Together

THE PROBLEM

Parents today feel guilty about missing out on raising their baby and experiencing key development milestones when they return back to work post parental leave.

THE SOLUTION

A two-part solution which allows parents to immersively interact with their babies when at work through reading a book, singing a song and capturing memories while also trackind baby development through a voice assistant and automatic milestone detection.

Reading & Singing to your Baby

This feature allows parents to read a book or sing a song with their baby via video chatting.

Tracking Baby Development

This feature allows parents to determine whether their baby is hitting development goals according to known points through the voice assistant.

Automated Milestone Detection

This feature allows parents to see in the moment important development milestones as detected through artificial intelligence.

Saving and Viewing Memories

This feature allows parents to save and view photos taken during their baby's development and video calls.
