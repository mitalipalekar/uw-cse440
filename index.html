Bringing Parents and Babies Closer Together

THE PROBLEM

Parents today feel guilty about missing out on raising their baby and experiencing key development milestones when they return back to work post parental leave.

THE SOLUTION

A two-part solution which allows parents to immersively interact with their babies when at work through reading a book, singing a song and capturing memories while also trackind baby development through a voice assistant and automatic milestone detection.
